About

Eastern India’s Volleyball Pride

Kolkata Thunderbolts (KTB) is India’s top men’s volleyball team from Kolkata, West Bengal playing in the Prime Volleyball League - the country’s premier tournament. The team was founded in 2021 by Kolkata Sports Ventures with the aim to take the sport to masses, engage them, and create a volleyball ecosystem in Bengal and nearby regions. Kolkata Sports Ventures are also involved in various CSR initiatives for children, using volleyball as a medium for education. KTB won the inaugural edition of the PVL in 2022 and became the league champions in 2023. With a championship winning mindset, KTB strides onto the upcoming Rupay Prime Volleyball League Season 3 powered by A23.
